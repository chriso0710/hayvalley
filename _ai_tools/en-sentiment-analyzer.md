Real-time AI analysis for event feedback across all channels. Attendees send text or images during a session — the AI classifies every response instantly: sentiment scoring, automatic blocking of inappropriate content, and smart routing to human editors when needed.

The output is structured and ready for downstream use: summaries, keywords, word clouds, and sentiment scores per session. Works with multi-channel input (email, WhatsApp, web) and supports image analysis for social walls.
