## What Level Is Your Team On?

There are 8 levels of AI-assisted development — where do you stand?

1. **No AI** — Traditional development without AI tooling
2. **Agent in IDE, Permissions On** — AI suggests, you approve every change
3. **YOLO Mode** — Agent runs freely, trust is rising
4. **Diffs Fade, Conversation Leads** — You stop reviewing every diff and focus on guiding the agent
5. **Agent-First, IDE Later** — You work in the agent conversation, the IDE is just where you look at the code
6. **Agent Multiplexing** — Multiple agents run simultaneously, you manage parallel streams
7. **10+ Agents, Managed by Hand** — Coordination becomes the challenge
8. **Build Your Own Orchestrator** — You write the coordination layer, spawning and managing agents programmatically

Most teams are at Level 1–3. I help you get to Level 5 and beyond.
