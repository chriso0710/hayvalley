45 minutes of talk, digestible in 90 seconds. Event summarizer that creates transcripts from video and audio via AI. Organizers determine the type of summary (length, format, style, language).

The AI extracts everything relevant from a transcript:

- Topic extraction
- Key takeaways
- Quotes
- Action items
- Mentioned tools and products
- Numbers and statistics
- Named persons and companies
- Audience Q&A
- Speaker recommendations (books, links, resources)
- Sentiment analysis
- Auto-generated FAQ
- Glossary of technical terms
- Short and long versions

Results are available via API as structured JSON or presented in a branded media library in the customer's corporate design — a complete, searchable archive of every talk, ready to share right after the event.

Optionally generates a personalized post-event magazine for each attendee, curated based on their interests and session history.
