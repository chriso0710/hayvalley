Privacy Policy

1. Privacy at a Glance

The following information provides a simple overview of what happens to your personal data when you visit this website.

2. General Information

The operators of this website take the protection of your personal data very seriously. We treat your personal data confidentially and in accordance with the statutory data protection regulations and this privacy policy.

3. Hosting

This website is hosted on Netlify. For details on how your data is handled, please refer to Netlify's privacy policy: netlify.com/privacy.

4. Contact Form

If you send us inquiries via the contact form, your details from the form, including the contact data you provide, will be stored for the purpose of processing the inquiry and in case of follow-up questions. We do not share this data without your consent. Processing is handled via Netlify Forms.

5. External Services

This website uses the following external services:

  • Google Fonts — Fonts are loaded from Google servers. Your IP address is transmitted to Google. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Bootstrap CDN (jsDelivr) — The CSS framework is loaded via a CDN.
  • Font Awesome (cdnjs) — Icons are loaded via a CDN.

6. Your Rights

You have the right at any time to obtain free information about the origin, recipient, and purpose of your stored personal data. You also have the right to request the correction or deletion of this data. For this purpose, as well as for further questions on the subject of data protection, you can contact us at any time.
